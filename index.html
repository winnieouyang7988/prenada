What is Preñada?

A Brief Introduction of Our App.

Preñada is an app that aims to help women relieve anxiety during the process of pregnancy since they spend a lot of effort to acquire accurate information and hope to give birth in the best condition.

Product Features

What we aim to achieve.

Customized Checklist

Preñada will provide a weekly checklist to help the users complete their pregnant-related mission and thus lessen their concerns.

Body Data Tracking

Prenñada will record the user's mental and physical data to optimize the recommendation content.

Knowledge Recomendations

Preñada will push knowledge across all domains referencing the user's pregnancy progress and personal data.

Design Process

What we have done to make this happend.

Needfinding

At first, we came up with many ideas from different domains. After picking some criteria and voting by order of importance, we finally chose to create a pregnancy experience sharing platform!

We then gained valuable insights after interviewing three people with different occupations. We found out that it is necessary to help pregnant women and their partners because of the information overflow and the uncertainty of data correctness.

Read the PDF Slides

POVs + HMWs

Initially, we set our mission as improving the sharing process of the pregnancy experience and involving the accompaniers' role. After narrowing down our direction by utilizing the feedback from three parents, we discovered that it would be game-changing if parents could easily retrieve comprehensive information and if mothers could make decisions more confidently.

We then broke the two POVs into several HMWs to list all the problems we could consider solving and brain-storming several solutions to crack all those issues.

Finally, we aim to enhance partner interaction by creating an exchange album, pairing users up to encourage experience sharing, etc.

Concept Confirmation

After gaining feedback from the class and the professor, we rethought our project goal and tried to make the solution available for the app. Also, we investigated our potential competitors to discover our unique selling points.

Eventually, We set our value proposition as "Relieve Pregnancy Anxiety" by providing the user with a pregnant event related calendar, recommendations by weeks, and a knowledge library.

We then made the concept video according to our solutions and value proposition, and we also separated the solutions into three tasks. In the section below, we present you with the concept video.

Experience Prototype

After converging on the app's purpose and deeply designing the function of our features, we started brainstorming different UI design ideas and drew sketches to choose the final candidate. We wireframed a task flow for each task and completed the final prototype structure. Then, we made a paper prototype according to our design and asked three participants to join our usability test.

In our experiment, we would have a brief explanation of the whole concept first and ask them to do three tasks using our paper prototype. In our investigation, we would first have a short description of the entire concept and ask them to do three tasks using our paper prototype. Finally, we revised our design according to their feedback.
